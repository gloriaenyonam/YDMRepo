About Youth Discovery Movement(YDM)

ABOUT US

To discover is to see, find or become aware of something for the first time. For that matter, we bring Ghanaian youth together as a group, family and community to be nurtured on emerging technology in the world today.

WHAT WE DO

It's an entire package for the future. Our main aim is to plug Ghana into the technological world. Together with experts in the technological field, we bring out fully nurtured talents and make dreams become a reality which would make a difference in the world to come.

Technology is best when it brings people together. ~ Matt Mullenweg
