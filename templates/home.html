YDM TECH EXPO

Coming September 2023, we are bringing together the brightest minds to witness as we showcase latest technologies such as "Virtuality Reality Devices, the BBC Microbit and the Maqueen Robot" amongst others. We are inviting techies to also grace the event to inspire participants and the world at large to take up careers in IT. The objectives of this Expo are;

  • To witness breathtaking technologies
  • To connect with like-minded individuals
  • To engage in thought provoking discussions
  • To enjoy the scenery of our exhibit hall which is packed with latest advancements from artificial intelligence to robotics, and from blockchain to augmented reality, and literally every field

Our key-note speakers are some of the most influential figures in the tech world who would share their insights on the current state and future direction of technology.
So mark your calendars and join us for Tech Expo'23- a must-attend event for us all. SEE YOU THERE!
